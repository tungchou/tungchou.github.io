Achievements

  • I am one of the authors of Classic McEliece, a post-quantum key encapsulation mechanism (KEM). The scheme has been recommended by BSI for long-term confidentiality and is currently one of the few candidates in the 4th round of NISTPQC. I am the main programmer of the 4 official software implementations of the scheme. I am also the one who designed the 5 'f' parameter sets, which allow faster key generation than other parameter sets.

  • I am one of the authors of MEDS, a post-quantum signature scheme. I designed 2 algebraic modellings for the underlying hard problem; see the paragraph "improved modelling" of the corresponding paper. I also designed a way to reduce the signature size by a large factor; see Section 8 of the specification.

  • I am one of the authors of the XL implementation shown in Ruben Niederhagen's webpage. The implementation has been used to break Fukuoka MQ challenges and Rainbow.

  • I am one of the authors of libFES, which is an early version of libFES-lite, a library for solving polynomial systems over F2 via exhaustive search.
